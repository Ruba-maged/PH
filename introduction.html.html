Welcome to PH !

What is PH?

We mean to create a medical website, that raises awareness of some unknown diseases. We aim to be a part of their healing process, as supporters and guiders. Our targeted users are:

    •People with disabilities/ diseases/ rare syndromes.
    •Medical experts/ scientists/ doctors.
    •Donators.

Why was PH created?

There are a lot of brutal unacknowledged diseases, people who suffer from those diseases may find it difficult to cope with, these diseases may include:

    • Ochoa syndrome
    • Foreign accent syndrome
    • Carcinoid syndrome
    • Situs inversus
    • Wilson’s disease
    • Stiff person syndrome
    • Laughing Death ( Kuru ).

And many more.

Many countries may not have the qualified doctors or facilities to help those in need of a treatment. We try as much as possible to help them, by providing the required finances based off donations, and organizing meetings with expert doctors

How we can help:

We’re aiming to create a website that helps them cope in various ways;

    •Group therapy: people with the same conditions and symptoms can help each other face whatever they are going through.
    •Motivation: we connect those who still suffer from a disease to cured ones , so they can be a source of inspiration to them.
    •Experts’ advice: Some diseases and disabilities are hard to cure and it’s not always that a specialist is available, we try to provide that.

Not only that, but we will provide side options as well, that could play small roles in healing those people;

    •Medical tests: Tests designed by experts to help people determine whether they have a certain syndrome or disease or not, according to their symptoms and medical tests.
    • Collecting donations.
    • Detecting nearby hospitals that require organ donations.
    • Provided medical reports: Reports that describe certain diseases and their symptoms to help patients learn more about them.
